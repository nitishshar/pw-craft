About Us

pw-craft is a Playwright-first wrapper library that bundles sensible defaults, expressive helpers, and a reporting layer you can actually ship to humans.

  • Browser lifecycle management with opinionated configuration
  • Navigation, waiting, actions, assertions, storage, network, media, and accessibility utilities
  • Cucumber world wiring so BDD suites stay short and readable
  • Self-contained HTML reports with charts and filters
